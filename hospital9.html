Facilities

Maternity and Pediatric Care: Dedicated wards with advanced facilities for maternal care and pediatric treatment.
Cancer Care Services: Comprehensive oncology treatments including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical interventions.
Transplant Services: Specialized units for organ transplants such as kidney and liver transplants.
Advanced Imaging: Cutting-edge imaging technologies including MRI, CT scan, and digital radiography.
Cardiac Care: State-of-the-art cardiac units for diagnosis and treatment of heart diseases.
Surgical Services: Advanced surgical suites equipped for complex surgeries across various specialties.
Rehabilitation: Comprehensive rehabilitation services for post-operative and chronic condition recovery.
Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency services with specialized trauma care and critical care units.
Patient Support: Dedicated patient support services including counseling and rehabilitation programs.
Medical Research: Active participation in medical research and clinical trials to advance healthcare.

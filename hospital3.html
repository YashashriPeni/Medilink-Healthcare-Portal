Overview

Yashoda Hospitals are renowned for their advanced medical facilities and specialized care across diverse disciplines such as cardiology, oncology, and neurology. With a focus on patient-centric services, they provide state-of-the-art diagnostic technology, expert medical teams, and a commitment to compassionate healthcare delivery. Facilities

Maternity and Pediatric Care: Dedicated maternity wards and pediatric departments equipped with neonatal intensive care units (NICU) and pediatric intensive care units (PICU).

Cancer Care Services: Comprehensive oncology services including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology.

Transplant Services: Organ transplant programs such as kidney, liver, and bone marrow transplants with specialized transplant units.

Advanced Imaging Services: High-resolution imaging modalities like PET-CT scan and digital mammography for accurate diagnostics.

Pain Management Clinics: Specialized pain management clinics offering advanced treatments for chronic pain conditions.

Wellness and Preventive Health: Health promotion and disease prevention programs including health check-ups and wellness screenings.

International Patient Services: Dedicated services for international patients including language interpreters, visa assistance, and accommodation arrangements.

Nutritional Counseling: Dietetics and nutrition services tailored to individual patient needs and medical conditions.

Research and Innovation: Active involvement in medical research, clinical trials, and healthcare innovation to advance treatment options and patient care.

Patient Education: Educational programs and resources for patients and their families to understand medical conditions, treatment options, and self-care practices.
